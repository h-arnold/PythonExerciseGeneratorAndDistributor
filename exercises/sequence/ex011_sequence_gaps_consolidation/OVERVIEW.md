# Teaching Notes: Sequence Gaps Consolidation

## Prerequisites
- Exercises ex002 through ex010 in the sequence strand
- Students should already know `print()`, variables, `input()`, arithmetic, and basic f-strings

## Common Misconceptions
- Leaving values as text when numbers are needed
- Forgetting to store a calculated value before printing it
- Reusing the wrong variable after `input()`
- Reaching for concatenation when a later task is meant to revisit f-strings

## Teaching Approach
1. Let students complete the single-line gaps quickly to rebuild confidence.
2. Slow down on the multi-line calculation tasks and ask them to trace the values.
3. Use the later tasks to connect earlier ideas into one complete program.

## Notes
- Keep hints focused on the missing line or lines, not the surrounding code.
- If students finish quickly, ask them to explain why each conversion is needed.
